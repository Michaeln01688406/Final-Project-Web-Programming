About Me

My name is Michael Bui, and I'm an international student from Vietnam currently studying Computer Programming and Analysis at Humber College. I moved to Canada during middle school, an experience that shaped me and taught me adaptability at a young age.

My journey into programming began in grade 11 when I was introduced to Java. That first exposure sparked my curiosity and solidified my passion for coding, leading me to pursue a future in technology. Since then, I've been dedicated to developing my skills and exploring the endless possibilities of programming.

Gaming has been a significant part of my life, both as a hobby and a source of inspiration. I hope to combine my love for gaming with my programming expertise and contribute to the world of game development in the future.

The experience of moving to a new country and adapting to a different culture has given me resilience and a unique perspective that I bring to everything I do. I'm excited to continue learning, growing, and contributing to the tech industry.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about me!
